Easy:

Minor popups, but only after 1:00pm or three hours of usage.


Medium:

Minor popups all day, regular popups after 1:00pm.
Forces regular popups after three hours of usage.


Hard:

Minor popups at the beginning of the day, regular popups after 11:00am, major popups after 2:00pm.
Popups appear more frequently.
Forces regular popups after one hour of usage.
Forces major popups after three hours of usage.


Torture:

Major popups all day, no matter what.
Locks your settings.




Minor popups: Small in-page popups. Popups appear every 10 minutes, and dissapear after a page reload.

Regular popup: Popup window that covers the screen. Appears every 10 minutes, and can easily be dismissed.

Major popup: Unavoidable popup that forces you to the Duolingo website. Appears constantly until a lesson is completed.
